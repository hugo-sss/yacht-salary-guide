- **Source:** Morgan & Mallet International
- **Data:** Detailed tables from small to mega yachts
- **Positions:** 50+ roles including specialists (carpenter, plumber, dive instructor)
- **Currency:** EUR & USD
- **Size Range:** Up to 90m+

### 5. `dockwalk-2024-salary-survey.md` ✅
- **Source:** Dockwalk

- **Type:** Agency ranges + poll ranges + averages
- **Currency:** USD & EUR

### 7. `uksa-2025-salary-guide.md` ✅
- **Source:** UKSA (UK Sailing Academy)
- **Data:** Career progression focus
- **Positions:** Core yacht crew positions
- **Currency:** EUR/month

### 8. `yachtbook-2026-salary-guide.md` ✅
- **Source:** Yachtbook / y@chtbook crew
- **Data:** Salary tables for exterior and interior crew
- **Positions:** Deck, engineering, interior, and galley roles
- **Currency:** EUR/month
- **Extras:** Freelance and delivery day rates

### 9. `crewfinders-yacht-starting-salaries.md` ✅
- **Source:** Crewfinders International
- **Data:** Starting salary table by vessel size
- **Positions:** Deck, engineering, culinary, and interior roles
- **Currency:** USD/year
- **Extras:** Feet-based yacht-size bands

### 10. `northrop-johnson-2023-salary-survey.md` ✅
- **Source:** Northrop & Johnson
- **Data:** Agency range, poll average, and poll range survey tables
- **Positions:** Deck, engineering, interior, and culinary roles
