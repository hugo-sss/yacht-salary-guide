# Quay Group - Superyacht Captain Salary & Leave Report 2025/26

**Source:** https://quaygroup.com/blog/superyacht-captain-salary-leave-report-2025-26/  
**Published:** November 17, 2025  
**Type:** Survey of 367 Captains on vessels 20m to 120m+

---

## Overview

Comprehensive report based on insights from 367 Captains working on vessels ranging from 20m to 120m+.

---

## Key Findings

### Work-Life Balance Issues
- **63%** of Captains now enjoy time-for-time rotation
- **62%** admit to performing some level of work during rotational leave
- Only **28%** completely switch off during leave
- **31%** work occasionally during leave
- **15%** work regularly during leave  
- **16%** work very frequently (several times per week)

### Salary Trends by Yacht Size

| Yacht Size | Average Monthly Pay | Notes |
|------------|-------------------|-------|
| 20-24m | ~€6,000 | Entry level |
| 70-79m | Increased 7% vs 2023 | Largest increase this year |
| 100-119m | €25,000+ | Top tier |

### Benefits & Perks

**Travel Perks:**
- More than half of Captains receive business-class travel on long-haul routes
- 4% increase from 2023 survey

**Pay Rises:**
- **53%** receive some form of annual pay rise
- Only **13%** have increases written into contracts

**Bonuses:**
- Many receive 13th-month bonus
- Others report inconsistent/unpredictable arrangements

### Job Satisfaction
- **37%** very satisfied in current roles
- ~25% feel neutral or dissatisfied

### Challenges Reported
- Excessive yacht usage
- Tight budgets  
- Crew turnover
- Unrealistic owner expectations

## Quote from Simon Ladbrooke (Captain Consultant)

> "The market is beginning to value experienced captains in a more meaningful way, but there's still more to do to make this career sustainable for the long term. On the positive side, this is still a fabulous career offering a great lifestyle, salary and benefits."
