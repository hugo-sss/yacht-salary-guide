## Captain Salary Ranges (Annual USD)

| Captain Level | Vessel Size | Salary Range |
|--------------|-------------|--------------|
| **Junior Captain** | 60 ft – 100 ft | $48,000 - $98,000 |
| **Captain** | 100 ft – 170 ft | $84,000 - $180,000 |
| **Senior Master** | 170 ft – 200 ft+ | $144,000 - $300,000+ |

### Monthly Breakdown (approximate)
- Junior Captain: $4,000 - $8,167/month
- Captain: $7,000 - $15,000/month
- Senior Master: $12,000 - $25,000+/month

## Salary Modifiers

### Positive Modifiers (Increase Pay)
- Speaking multiple languages
- Additional certifications
- Specialized skills
- Experience in specific cruising areas
- Longevity with yacht/owner

### Negative Modifiers (Decrease Pay)
- Limited experience in intended cruising area
- Lack of specific certifications
- No longevity with program
