# Dockwalk 2025 Superyacht Crew Salary Survey

**Source:** https://www.dockwalk.com/jobs/salary-guide  
**Published:** June 2, 2025  
**Type:** Industry survey with real crew feedback

---

## Overview

Dockwalk's annual salary survey gathers real-time salary feedback from thousands of crewmembers and crew agencies worldwide.

### Key Findings 2024
- Crew wages largely flat compared to previous year
- Salaries increased in 2021-2022 when crew were in short supply
- More crew available now, increasing competition
- Crew demanding higher pay but not necessarily receiving it

### Data Format
The survey provides three data points for each position:
1. **Agency Range:** Average lows and highs from all agencies
2. **Poll Range:** Lowest and highest individual responses
3. **Poll Average:** Calculated from all responses

*Note: Figures do not account for longevity, experience, benefits, tips, or extra remuneration.*
