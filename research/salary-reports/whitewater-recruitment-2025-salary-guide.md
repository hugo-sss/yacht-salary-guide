## Overview

This PDF is Whitewater Recruitment's `Superyacht Crew Salary Guide 2025`. It presents monthly salary benchmarks and typical leave structures across the main yacht departments, followed by a short market-trends section.

Whitewater frames the guide as a salary benchmark for both crew and employers, based on its internal industry data, placement records, and market analysis.

## Structure Of The Guide

Each salary entry combines:

- a monthly salary range in euros
- a typical leave/rotation pattern

Examples of leave wording in the PDF include:

- `45-60 days`
- `60-90 days`
- `90 days+`
- `Equal rotation`
- `3:1 rotation`
- `Non-rotational`
- `Discretionary`

That means this source is useful both for salary benchmarking and for future leave-structure comparisons.

## Market Commentary Pages

Pages 7-8 summarize Whitewater's stated market themes for 2025:

- specialist skills commanding premium pay
- retention and crew longevity becoming more important
- stronger professionalism and scrutiny in hiring
- sustainability experience becoming more valuable

These pages are useful for context but are not part of the structured salary table.
